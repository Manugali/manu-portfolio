My site already explains what I do for a living. The frameworks, the integrations, the enterprise systems — that story lives on the other pages.

I do not need another place to repeat it.

I wanted somewhere quieter. Not announcements. Not tutorials. Not another person on the internet explaining how to be productive.

Just writing, when something sticks.

That might be a week that felt strange. A place I miss. A thought I cannot shake on the drive home. Nothing polished. Nothing trying to teach anyone anything.

If you landed here from my portfolio — hello. I am Manohar. The professional version of me is one click away. This is the other version. Still figuring out how much of it belongs on the internet.

More posts when they are ready. Not on a schedule.
